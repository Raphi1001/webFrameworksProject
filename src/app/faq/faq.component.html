Game

Where do I play it?

Click on the Game button, you should be able to play it now.

What is the Goal?

Set the picture in the correct order as fast as possible and with the least amount of tries.

How do I move the move the pictures?

Left-click on the picture. If you did it correctly, it should look different. If you left-click on a second picture now, they switch places.

How do I win?

If you set every picture in the correct order, a message should appear beneath the puzzle that says "Solved", and the timer stops.

How do I get a Score?

You have to log in to get your score. We calculate your score via this formula: 100 – seconds to solve the Puzzle = score, and no, you can't get a negative score.

It is your highest Scores ever achived.

My Email is already used?

It appears you already created an Account, try to log in via the Login Tab.

My passwords don't match?

We want to make sure that you didn't misclick while trying to type in your new password. To fix it, retype your password in both fields.
